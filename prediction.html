my prediction for 2022's sneaker trends

these are all my predictions of which i think will be big/and are already starting to become sneaker trends starting from a few specifics for this winter, then transitioning to spring, summer and fall. a lot (most) of my shoe predictions are pink because it's more in style now in my opinion too.

  • mini bailey bow ii ugg boot

    • these ugg boots have taken a toll on teen girls and young adults seen in a certain aesthetic because of its look and colour, called 'coquette aesthetic'. they're cute, iconic and such a good basic for the winter and fall. they're already selling out in the pink since it's the most popular.

  • moon boot

    • to be honest, when i first saw these i had a feeling they would become popular because of the style. i wasn't into them at first until i saw a few celebrities such as dua lipa and hailey bieber in them and now i have a feeling they might catch on as a trend soon enough.

  • platform soled sneakers

    • it's not the most specific prediction but i feel like all sorts of sneakers, boots, etc in general are starting to make a comeback like platform combat boots, loafers, converse, nikes and especially since these nike dunk rebels that i am pretty sure has been dropped this year, they'll become trendy this 2022.

  • designer sneakers

    • there are so many designer brands that released sneakers last year collabing with nike, converse, etc and lots were quite the rage so maybe they'll be back again this year!
